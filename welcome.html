Basketball 101

(Rules, Gameplay, Stats, Court, Positions, Fouls, Violations, Analytics)

1️⃣ The Court — Everything to Know

  • Three-point line – shots behind it = 3 pts
  • Free-throw line – where fouled players shoot
  • Paint / Key – rectangle under the basket
  • Low post / High post – inside scoring zones
  • Baseline / Sideline – out of bounds
  • Half-court line – prevents going backwards
  • Restricted circle – no charges allowed under the rim

Dimensions

  • Court: 94 ft × 50 ft
  • Hoop: 10 ft high
  • Free throw: 15 ft
  • 3-point line NBA: 22–23.75 ft
  • 3-point line NCAA men: 22 ft

2️⃣ ️Players & Positions ⛹️‍♂️⛹️‍♀️

1. Point Guard (PG) – “Floor General”
Controls offense • Dribbles most • Calls plays • Pass-first

2. Shooting Guard (SG) – “Scorer”
Best shooter • Makes 3s • Midrange • Drives

3. Small Forward (SF) – “All-Around”
Shoots • Drives • Defends • Versatile

4. Power Forward (PF) – “Inside/Outside Big”
Rebounds • Screens • Paint scoring • Midrange

5. Center (C) – “Rim Protector”
Tallest • Shot blocks • Rebounds • Close shots / Dunks

3️⃣ Game Structure 🏟️

  • NBA: 4 × 12-min quarters
  • NCAA Men: 2 × 20-min halves
  • NCAA Women: 4 × 10-min quarters
  • Shot clock: NBA 24s • NCAA 30s
  • Shot clock resets to 14 after hitting rim
  • Jump ball starts game
  • OT: 5 minutes each

4️⃣ How to Score 🔥

Free Throws 1 point — fouled while shooting
Layup 2 points — close-range finish
Dunk 2 points — strong finish
Jump Shot 2 points — inside the arc
Three-pointer 3 points — outside the arc
Putback 2 points — after rebound
And-1 Score + 1 free throw

5️⃣ Basic Offense 🎯

  • Pick-and-roll — guard uses a screen from a big
  • Isolation (ISO) — 1v1 scoring
  • Fast break — running after a rebound or steal
  • Spacing — players around the 3-point line opening lanes
  • Motion offense — cuts, screens, constant movement

6️⃣ Basic Defense 🛡️

  • Man-to-man
  • Zones: 2–3, 3–2, 1–3–1, 2-1-2, 1-2-2
  • Help defense & rotations
  • Press defense (full court)

7️⃣ Fouls 🚫

  • Personal — pushing, reaching, blocking, charging
  • Shooting foul — free throws
  • Offensive foul — charge, illegal screen
  • Bonus — automatic free throws after foul limit
  • Technical — arguing, taunting
  • Flagrant — dangerous or excessive contact

8️⃣ Violations 🚫

  • Traveling
  • Double dribble
  • Carry / Palming
  • Shot clock violation
  • 5-second inbound violation
  • 8-second backcourt violation
  • 3 seconds in the paint
  • Backcourt violation
  • Out of bounds

9️⃣ Rebounding 🧱

  • Offensive rebound — extend possession
  • Defensive rebound — end opponent possession
  • Box out — seal defender before the ball arrives
  • Use two hands to secure the ball
  • Explode upward — timing matters more than height

🔟 Key Basketball Stats ⭐

Basic Stats

  • PTS — Points
  • REB — Rebounds
  • AST — Assists
  • STL — Steals
  • BLK — Blocks
  • TO — Turnovers
  • PF — Personal Fouls
  • FG% • 3P% • FT%

Advanced Shots

  • FGM/FGA — Field goals made/attempted
  • 3PM/3PA — 3-pointers made/attempted
  • FTM/FTA — Free throws made/attempted
  • EFF — Efficiency rating (simple)

Advanced Analytics

  • PER — Player Efficiency Rating
  • TS% — True Shooting %
  • eFG% — Effective Field Goal %
  • USG% — Usage Rate
  • Off/Def Rating — Points per 100 possessions
  • +/- — Impact on the game
  • Win Shares — Contribution to wins

1️⃣1️⃣ Team Stats 🧢

Offense

  • PPP — Points per possession
  • Assist-to-turnover ratio
  • Fast break points
  • Second-chance points

Defense

  • Opponent FG%
  • Steals
  • Blocks
  • Paint points allowed
  • Defensive rebounding %

Style

  • Pace (how fast a team plays)
  • Shot selection (3s, midrange, layups)
  • Transition efficiency

1️⃣2️⃣ Flow of a Possession 🧩

  1. Defense rebounds or inbounds
  2. PG brings the ball up
  3. Offense sets spacing
  4. Screens or cuts happen
  5. Shot attempt
  6. Rebound or score
  7. Transition into defense

1️⃣3️⃣ Beginner Tips 🎥

  • Watch spacing
  • Watch screens
  • Watch help defense
  • Count possessions
  • Monitor shot clock usage
  • Track team fouls — bonus matters!
